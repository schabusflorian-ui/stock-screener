# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how Investment Research Platform ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our investment research platform.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help us:

- Remember your preferences and settings
- Keep you logged in
- Understand how you use the Service
- Improve your experience
- Analyze performance and usage patterns
- Provide personalized features

## Types of Technologies We Use

### Cookies
Small text files stored by your browser.

### Local Storage
Browser storage for larger amounts of data (e.g., cached market data, user preferences).

### Session Storage
Temporary storage cleared when you close your browser.

### Pixels/Beacons
Tiny images used for analytics and tracking.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|---------|----------|------|

### 3. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Service to improve it.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|---------|----------|------|
| _ga | Google Analytics - distinguish users | 2 years | Third-party |

**What we track with analytics:**
- Pages visited and features used
- Time spent on different sections
- User flow and navigation patterns
- Error rates and technical issues
- Device and browser information
- Geographic location (country/city level)

**What we DON'T track:**
- Personal financial information
- Specific securities in your portfolios
- Individual trading activities
- Keystrokes or form inputs

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|---------|----------|------|
| performance_metrics | Track page load times and errors | Session | First-party |
| feature_flags | Enable/disable experimental features | 7 days | First-party |

## Local Storage Usage

We use browser local storage for:

### Cached Market Data
- **Purpose**: Reduce API calls and improve performance
- **Data**: Public market data (prices, company info)
- **Retention**: 24 hours
- **Removable**: Yes, through browser settings

### User Preferences
- **Purpose**: Store settings across sessions
- **Data**: Display preferences, notification settings
- **Retention**: Until you clear browser data
- **Removable**: Yes, through Settings or browser

### Draft Content
- **Purpose**: Auto-save notes and queries
- **Data**: Unsaved watchlist notes, query drafts
- **Retention**: 7 days
- **Removable**: Yes, through browser settings

## How We Use Cookie Data

We use cookie data to:

1. **Provide the Service**: Authentication, session management, preferences
2. **Improve User Experience**: Remember settings, personalize interface
3. **Analyze Usage**: Understand feature adoption, identify issues
4. **Optimize Performance**: Monitor load times, identify bottlenecks
5. **Ensure Security**: Detect fraud, prevent abuse
6. **Product Development**: Inform feature development priorities

We do NOT:
- Sell cookie data to third parties
- Use cookies for advertising tracking across the web
- Share cookie data with data brokers
- Use cookies to track you outside our platform (except Google Analytics)

## Managing Cookies

### In-App Cookie Settings

You can manage your cookie preferences in:
**Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences**

Options:
- **Essential Only**: Required cookies only (minimum)
- **Essential + Functional**: Required + convenience features
- **All Cookies**: Full functionality and analytics

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  - [Chrome Help](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)

- **Firefox**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  - [Firefox Help](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies)

- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  - [Safari Help](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)

- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies
  - [Edge Help](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

### Clear Cookies

To delete existing cookies:
1. Use your browser's clear browsing data feature
2. Select "Cookies and site data"
3. Choose time range (e.g., "All time")
4. Confirm deletion

**Note**: Deleting cookies will log you out and reset your preferences.

### Opt-Out Links

- **Google Analytics**: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Network Advertising Initiative**: [https://optout.networkadvertising.org/](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
- **Digital Advertising Alliance**: [https://optout.aboutads.info/](https://optout.aboutads.info/)

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

### If You Disable All Cookies:
- You cannot log in or use the Service
- Settings will not be saved
- The platform may not function correctly

### If You Disable Optional Cookies:
- ✅ You can still use all core features
- ❌ Preferences won't be remembered across sessions
- ❌ We can't improve the platform based on your usage
- ❌ Some convenience features may be limited

## Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting. We currently do not respond to DNT signals because:
- There is no industry-wide standard for DNT compliance
- Implementation varies across browsers
- Legal requirements are unclear

Instead, we provide granular cookie controls in your Settings.
