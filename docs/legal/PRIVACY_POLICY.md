# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

Investment Research Platform ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our investment research platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address (from Google OAuth)
- **Profile Information**: Investment preferences, risk tolerance, preferred markets
- **User Content**: Watchlists, portfolios, notes, alerts, saved queries
- **Communications**: Support requests, feedback, feature requests
- **Natural Language Queries**: Questions and commands submitted to the AI system

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Features used, pages visited, time spent, query patterns
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, referring URLs, API requests
- **Cookies**: Session data, preferences (see Cookie Policy)
- **Performance Metrics**: Response times, error rates, system performance

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Google**: Profile information via OAuth (name, email, profile picture)
- **Market Data Providers**: Public market data associated with your queries
- **Analytics Providers**: Aggregated usage statistics

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide the Service**: Display data, save preferences, sync across devices
- **Personalize Experience**: Customize recommendations and insights
- **Improve AI Models**: Train and refine natural language understanding (anonymized)
- **Analyze Usage**: Understand feature usage and identify improvements
- **Communicate**: Send important updates, respond to inquiries
- **Ensure Security**: Detect fraud, protect against abuse, prevent unauthorized access
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements
- **Research & Development**: Improve algorithms, backtesting, and signal analysis

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your portfolio or watchlist data with other users (unless you choose to)
- Use your data for advertising to third parties
- Share your queries with market data providers

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

### 3.1 Service Providers
Third parties who help us operate the Service:
- Cloud hosting providers
- Analytics services

- Email delivery services
- AI/ML infrastructure providers

These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and process data only as instructed.

### 3.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by:
- Law or regulation

- Protection of our rights or property

### 3.3 Business Transfers
If we're involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

### 3.4 Aggregated Data
We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you individually:
- Usage statistics
- Market trend analysis
- Research findings

### 3.5 With Your Consent
We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:
- Your account is active
- Needed to provide the Service
- Required by law or regulation

After account deletion:
- Personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely
- Backups are purged within 90 days
- Cached data is cleared within 7 days

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 All Users
You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your personal data through Settings
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Delete**: Request account and data deletion
- **Export**: Download your data in JSON format
- **Withdraw Consent**: Opt out of optional data collection
- **Manage Cookies**: Control cookie preferences

### 5.2 European Users (GDPR)
If you're in the EU/EEA, you also have the right to:
- **Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Object**: Object to certain processing activities
- **Data Portability**: Receive data in a machine-readable format
- **Lodge Complaint**: File a complaint with your supervisory authority
- **Automated Decision Making**: Request human review of automated decisions

### 5.3 California Users (CCPA/CPRA)
If you're a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Know whether we sell or share personal information (we don't)
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights
- Correct inaccurate information
- Limit use of sensitive personal information

### 5.4 UK Users (UK GDPR)
UK users have the same rights as EU users under UK GDPR.

To exercise your rights:
- Use the Privacy Settings in your account
- Contact us at privacy@investmentresearchplatform.com
- Use the in-app data export/deletion tools

We will respond to requests within 30 days.

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption in Transit**: TLS 1.3 for all connections
- **Encryption at Rest**: Database and file storage encryption
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Security Monitoring**: Real-time threat detection
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and penetration testing
- **Secure Infrastructure**: Cloud providers with SOC 2 compliance
- **API Security**: Rate limiting, authentication, input validation

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you of any data breaches as required by law.

## 7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:
- **EU Standard Contractual Clauses** (SCCs)
- **UK International Data Transfer Agreement** (IDTA)
- **Adequacy Decisions**: Transfers to countries with adequate protection
- **Data Processing Agreements**: With all service providers

## 8. Children's Privacy
