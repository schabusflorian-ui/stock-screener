# AI Features Guide

This document explains the AI-powered features available in the Investment Platform and when to use each one.

---

## Quick Reference

| Feature | Location | Best For |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Ask AI | Research section | Research questions, comparisons, explanations |
| Trading Bots | Portfolio section | Automated trading strategies |
| AI Ratings | Company pages | Quick stock assessment |
| NL Query Bar | Global (Cmd+K) | Fast lookups, context-aware queries |
| Portfolio AI Signals | Portfolio header button | Position suggestions, rebalancing |

---

## Ask AI (formerly AI Analyst)

**Location:** Research > Ask AI (keyboard shortcut: G A)

**What it does:**
- Natural language questions about stocks, markets, and investment strategies
- Deep-dive analysis using multiple AI analysts (value, growth, quant, contrarian, tech, tail-risk)
- Comparisons between stocks or strategies
- Explanations of financial concepts and metrics

**Best use cases:**
- "What are the key risks for NVDA?"
- "Compare AAPL vs MSFT for a long-term investment"
- "Explain ROIC and why it matters"
- "Is now a good time to buy tech stocks?"

**Features:**
- Multiple specialist AI analysts with different perspectives
- Knowledge base integration with insights from famous investors
- Context-aware responses using your portfolio data

---

## Trading Bots (formerly Agents)

**Location:** Portfolio > Trading Bots (keyboard shortcut: G X)

**What it does:**
- Configurable automated trading strategies
- Meta-allocator that combines multiple strategy signals
- Paper trading mode for testing strategies
- Position sizing recommendations based on Kelly criterion

**Best use cases:**
- Setting up systematic trading rules
- Testing investment strategies before committing capital
- Running multiple strategies in parallel
- Getting rule-based trade recommendations

**Features:**
- Strategy configuration (momentum, mean-reversion, value, etc.)
- Backtesting against historical data
- Performance tracking and attribution
- Risk limits and position sizing

---

## AI Ratings (on Company Pages)

**Location:** Company page > Overview tab (hero section)

**What it does:**
- AI-generated investment rating (1-10 scale)
- Quick assessment combining multiple factors
- Rating history tracking over time
- Bullish/Neutral/Bearish classification

**Best use cases:**
- Quick screening of potential investments
- Getting a second opinion on a stock
- Tracking how AI sentiment changes over time

**Features:**
- Click to generate rating (not automatic to save costs)
- View historical ratings
- Refresh to get updated analysis
- Summary explanation of the rating

---

## NL Query Bar (Global Search)

**Location:** Any page (keyboard shortcut: Cmd+K or click search in sidebar)

**What it does:**
- Quick natural language queries from anywhere
- Context-aware responses
- Navigation and lookup
- Fast answers without leaving current page

**Best use cases:**
- "Show me AAPL" (navigation)
- "What's the P/E ratio of TSLA?" (quick lookup)
- "How is my portfolio performing?" (context-aware)
- "Find stocks with dividend yield > 4%" (screening)

**Features:**
- Works from any page
- Command palette integration
- Quick stock/investor/portfolio navigation
- Supports screening queries

---

## Portfolio AI Signals

**Location:** Portfolio page > "AI Signals" button in header ribbon

**What it does:**
- AI-powered recommendations for your specific portfolio
- Signal analysis based on your holdings
- Suggested trades and position adjustments
- Risk assessment and hedging suggestions

**Best use cases:**
- Getting trade ideas based on current holdings
- Reviewing AI recommendations before market open
- Checking for portfolio risks
- Finding rebalancing opportunities

**Features:**
- Latest signal view
- Performance tracking of past recommendations
- Pending trades queue
- Hedge suggestions
- Factor analysis
- Execution and risk settings

## AI Features Summary by Task

| Task | Recommended Feature |
|------|---------------------|
| Research a new stock | Ask AI + AI Rating |
| Get a quick stock opinion | AI Rating |
| Set up automated trading | Trading Bots |
| Ask about your portfolio | NL Query Bar or Portfolio AI Signals |
| Compare multiple stocks | Ask AI |
| Screen for opportunities | NL Query Bar |
| Get trade recommendations | Portfolio AI Signals |
| Test a strategy | Trading Bots (backtesting) |

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** - "What's AAPL's competitive moat?" is better than "Tell me about AAPL"

2. **Provide context** - If asking about your portfolio, mention your time horizon and risk tolerance

3. **Use the right tool** - Quick lookups are faster with NL Query Bar; deep research is better with Ask AI

4. **Check AI Ratings history** - The trend of ratings over time can be more informative than a single rating

5. **Paper trade first** - Use Trading Bots in paper trading mode before committing real capital
